Install FitExplorer

Get the best experience by installing FitExplorer on your device. It works offline and feels like a native app!

⬇️

Installation on iOS

Important: iOS requires you to manually add the app to your home screen. Follow these steps:
  1. Tap the Share button ︿ at the bottom of Safari
  2. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen"
  3. You'll see the FitExplorer icon and name
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
  5. Find the app icon on your home screen and tap it to open

Once installed, you'll see this bottom navigation:

Home
Workouts
Progress
Profile

Installation on Android

Tip: For Chrome on Android, you'll see an install prompt or can use the menu option.
  1. If you see an install banner at the bottom, tap "Install"
  2. Otherwise, tap the menu button (⋮) in the top right corner
  3. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete installation
  5. The app will appear on your home screen with its icon

Once installed, you'll see this bottom navigation:

Home
Workouts
Progress
