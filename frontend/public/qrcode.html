Install FitExplorer on your iPhone

Scan this QR code with your iPhone camera to install FitExplorer as a mobile app.

Installation Steps:

  1. Scan the QR code with your iPhone camera
  2. Tap the notification that appears
  3. When the website loads, tap the Share button
  4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  5. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
  6. FitExplorer will now appear on your home screen as an app!

FitExplorer gives you a complete fitness tracking experience directly from your home screen. Track workouts, plan routines, monitor nutrition, and more!
